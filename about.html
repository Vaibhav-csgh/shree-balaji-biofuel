About Us

A Revolutionary Step Towards Sustainable Energy

At Shree Balaji BioFuels, we believe in building a sustainable future through collaboration. Our ethanol plant will be a cooperative-driven initiative—procurement, operations, and management will be conducted through Venkateshwara Co-operative Power and Agro Processing Ltd. What makes us unique is the leadership of ex-servicemen, who bring discipline, commitment, and integrity to every aspect of the plant's operations—from agriculture support to raw material procurement and plant management.

Cooperative-Driven

Our operations are managed through transparent cooperative structures, ensuring fair participation and shared prosperity.

Ex-Servicemen Leadership

Military precision and integrity guide our operations, bringing discipline and excellence to every process.

Sustainable Focus

Environmental stewardship is at the core of our mission, driving innovation in clean energy solutions.

Our Vision

One Goal – Prosperity Through Cooperation

Establish India as a global leader in sustainable biofuel production, reducing carbon emissions by 85% compared to fossil fuels and supporting climate resilience. Create a farmer-first ecosystem ensuring fair pricing, direct market access, and increased income through transparent cooperative operations. Contribute to India's Ethanol Blending Programme and reduce dependency on crude oil imports, strengthening national energy security.
