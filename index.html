FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our mission and operations.

What is Shree Balaji Biofuels mission?

Our mission is to produce sustainable, grain-based ethanol (200 KLPD) using maize and rice, empowering farmers through a cooperative model and contributing to Indias energy security with eco-friendly fuel.

How does the cooperative model work?

We operate through Venkateshwara Co-operative Power and Agro Processing Ltd., involving farmers in procurement, operations, and management. This ensures fair pricing, direct market access, and support like high-quality seeds and agri-inputs.

Who manages the ethanol plant?

The plant is led by ex-servicemen, bringing discipline and integrity to operations, from raw material procurement to production, ensuring operational excellence and social responsibility.

How does Shree Balaji Biofuels support farmers?

We provide agri-input support, high-quality seeds, transparent pricing, and direct procurement without middlemen, ensuring fair income and sustainable farming practices.

What environmental impact does your ethanol production have?

Our grain-based ethanol is eco-friendly, reducing carbon emissions and promoting cleaner fuel alternatives, contributing to Indias energy independence and sustainability goals.
